How It Works

Discover how our AI segments your meal and analyzes macronutrients using advanced computer vision.

Segmentation Model

Our model identifies and separates different food items in your meal photo. Each segment is analyzed for its macronutrient content, providing a detailed breakdown.

Training Dataset

We use the FoodSeg103 dataset, a large collection of annotated food images, to train our model. This ensures accurate recognition of a wide variety of foods.

Deep Learning Architecture

Our segmentation model is based on the UNet architecture with a ResNet50 encoder. This combination enables precise food segmentation and robust feature extraction.
